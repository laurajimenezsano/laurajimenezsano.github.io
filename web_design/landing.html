Summary: Latitude vs. X

In this project, our goal is to create a website using HTML and CSS to show mutiple visualizations derived from a dataset on latitude and longitude. There will be a landing page, and within the landing page to the right side, you will see images linked to their own visualization pages.

Furthermore, I have created a comparison page for you to look at all graphs side by side and compare latitude against one of our four variables: Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness and Wind.

In addition, the data tab witin the navigation bar contains the dataset used, in case you would like to review it yourself!

Hope you enjoy it!
